Lefty's

Advantages

The advantages of being left-handed are plentiful, advantages such as: being unique as only ten percent of the
population is left-handed, and reportedly recovering from strokes quicker as lefty's constantly strengthen both
sides of the brain. More advantages include having an upper hand in sports where opposing players are more
accustomed to right-handed opponents, and reportedly being able to solve complex problems more efficiently
by more readily using both sides of the brain.

Disadvantages

The advantages might be pleantiful, but so are the disadvantages, with a small percentage of the population being
left-handed, a lot of everyday objects are made for right-handed people. Objects such as power tools, playing cards,
and most school desks. Another disadvantage includes handwriting, the english language is written and read, from
top to bottom, and left to right; as a consequence, left-handed people smudge and smear what they have already
written, as if the writing wasn't already unlegible.
